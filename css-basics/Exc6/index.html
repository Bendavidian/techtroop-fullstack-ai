Classic Pancakes

Soft, golden pancakes for a simple breakfast or weekend brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Preparation

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Whisk the milk, egg, and melted butter in another bowl.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Cook small portions on a hot pan until both sides are golden.
